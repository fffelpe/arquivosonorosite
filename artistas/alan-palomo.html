Biografia (resumo)

Alan Palomo is a singer-songwriter, producer, and DJ who created and front the project Neon Indian, which released three studio albums and appeared on The Tonight Show. The project Neon Indian officially concluded in 2023 and Alan Palomo is now performing under his own name. Additionally, he scored the films Lace Crater (2015), Everything Beautiful Is Far Away (2017), and Relaxer (2018), and appeared in Song to Song (2017). He has directed two music videos for Neon Indian. Read more on Last.fm
